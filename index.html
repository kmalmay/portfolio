The Process

Exploration: This phase of the project is all about setting up for success. To start off, you'll fill out a brief questionnaire so that I can get a feel for your project needs. It’ll take no more than 5 minutes, but will give me an idea of what you’re looking for.


After I’ve reviewed the questionnaire, we’ll chat about your project goals, brand, and any other information related to your project. I’ll send you an agreement outlining the details we’ve discussed, and I will revise it as necessary to ensure we’re both on the same page. Once the agreement is signed and squared away, we’ll get started!

Creation: In this phase I’ll dive into creating the project. I’ll begin by conducting my own research and using information from our discussions in the exploration phase to produce visuals that relay the look and feel of the future product. Then, I will begin creating a design.


Each step of the way I will communicate my progress and anything I may need from you to keep the project running smoothly and on time. At the milestones we’ve agreed upon, I will share the design for your feedback. Once we’re happy with the design, I will finalize it and develop the product.

Launch & Follow Up: The design achieves the goals we set in phase one, it’s functionality has been tested, and we’re ready to launch!


Once I launch the site, I will directly send all of the deliverables to you, including images, designs, and anything else discussed in our agreement. These now belong to you, and I’ll answer any questions you have about how to store or use the files in the future.


Lastly, I’d love to get project feedback from you so that I can continue to improve my process and my work!
