Summary

My goal for this project was to design the entire landing page concept from scratch, and then code my design into a responsive web page using the user stories found on FreeCodeCampâ€™s project requirements as guidelines

First, I decided on a brief. An online company providing photo storage, organization, and printing services to a target audience of couples and families. I started by brainstorming the information I would need on the page, then created some wireframe ideas for the page layout.

Since photos are generally taken during exciting moments to look back on, I chose bright colors and a playful header fonts to create an energetic feel. I created a photo collage image for the page background in Adobe XD and placed a transparent background over it so as not to distract from the foreground.

After coding the project I posted my project for feedback on the FreeCodeCamp forum where I got a lot of great recommendations and questions. I chose to implement several of the recommendations on my webpage.

The landing page includes: navigation at the top of the page with a hamburger menu appearing at mobile size, a place to sign up for the service at the top highlighted at the of the page, descriptions of the services offered, an overview of the pricing plans available, a video to engage the audience in the services provided, and a footer for additional information.
